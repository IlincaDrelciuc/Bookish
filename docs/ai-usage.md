## How AI was used in this project

### 1. Architecture decisions
The AI was consulted when discussing technology and architecture decisions,
such as the three-tier client-server structure, JWT authentication approach,
and database schema design. Final decisions were made by the author.

### 2. Debugging and code review
The AI assistant was occasionally consulted during the implementation phase
to help identify bugs and suggest fixes for specific technical issues.
All code was written and integrated by the author.

### 3. Thesis writing assistance
The AI was used to help refine and clarify written sections based on detailed
notes and content provided by the author. All thesis content reflects the
author's own research, decisions, and findings.

### 4. User study preparation
The AI helped format the SUS questionnaire and structure the task script
based on the author's own study design.

---

## What AI was NOT used for

- The original project idea and motivation
- Writing the recommendation algorithms
- Conducting the user study
- Interpreting results and drawing conclusions
- Academic references and citations

## Notes

AI was used selectively as an architectural consultant, debugging aid,
